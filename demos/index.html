Cartolina JS — demos

Simple terrain
Global terrain with hillshading and no texture overlay. Minimal style; good starting point.
Complex terrain
Illumination, bump maps, specular maps, and point labels. Renders the Grossglockner area.
Map — generic style loader
Parametrised demo. Accepts style=, pos=, and backend= URL parameters; supports __placeholder__ expansion in style JSON.
Relief Lab
Interactive controls for illumination, shading weights, atmosphere, vertical exaggeration, and render flags. Live readback from renderer state.
Depth test
Elevation and depth overlay diagnostic.
Waypoint
Geographic story map. Keyboard navigation flies between JSON-configured positions; image markers stay anchored to geographic coordinates. Embeddable as a plain ES module (e.g. in reveal.js).
